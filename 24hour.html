Appliances & Utilities

  • Turn off ice makers (freezer and inside refrigerator).
  • Set A/C to 70 degrees.
  • Turn off main water valve.

Household Items

  • Put towels and sheets in the laundry room.
  • Take out all trash.

Doors & Security

  • Ensure all 3 sliding glass doors are locked.
  • Secure windows by making sure they are locked.
  • Double-check exterior doors to ensure they are locked.
  • Turn off all lights.

Kitchen & Refrigerator

  • Remove perishable food from the refrigerator and freezer.
  • Empty the dishwasher.

Cleaning Tasks

  • Clean the microwave, stove, refrigerator, and dishwasher.
