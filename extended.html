Appliances & Utilities

  • Turn off ice makers (freezer and inside refrigerator).
  • Turn off the main water valve.
  • Turn off the hot water heater.
  • Set A/C to 74 degrees.
  • Turn off lights
  • Run all ceiling fans
  • Empty Fridge and Freezer
  • Empty Dishwasher

Household Items

  • Put towels and sheets in the laundry room.
  • Take out all trash.

Doors & Security

  • Ensure all 3 sliding glass doors are locked.
  • Secure windows by making sure they are locked.
  • Turn off all lights.
  • Set the security alarm (if we get one).
  • Close the Hurricane Shutters for any length June-Nov
  • Close the Hurricane Shutters if leaving for 3+ days not in Hurricane Season

Kitchen & Refrigerator

  • Remove perishable food from the refrigerator and freezer.
  • Empty the dishwasher.
  • Open dishwasher and washing machine doors.
  • Unplug small appliances (e.g., coffee maker, toaster, blender).

Cleaning Tasks

  • Clean the microwave, stove, refrigerator, dishwasher, and washing machine.
  • Clean furniture.

Bathroom & Storage

  • Open shower doors to allow air circulation.
  • Open closet and cabinet doors.

Additional Maintenance

  • Check smoke detectors to ensure they are operational.
  • Check that no faucets are dripping or running.
  • Store outdoor furniture or cushions indoors.
