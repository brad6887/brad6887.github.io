Noise Policies

  • Keep noise levels to a minimum, especially between 10 PM and 8 AM.
  • Be considerate of neighbors when using common areas.

Pool Rules

  • Pool hours are from dawn to dusk. Shower before entering the pool.
  • No animals, glass containers, or smoking allowed in the pool area.
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. No horseplay, running, or diving.
  • Personal flotation devices and swim fins are allowed if used respectfully.
  • All food and drinks must be at least 4 feet away from the pool edge and in non-glass containers.
  • Leave the pool area immediately during lightning or severe weather.
  • Proper swimwear is required, and children under 3 or those not toilet-trained must use plastic wading pools.

Tennis Courts

  • The tennis courts are available from dawn to dusk.
  • During the peak season, you may reserve court time by signing up at the office.

Pet Policy

  • No pets are allowed in the condo.

General Conduct

  • Respect shared spaces and leave them as clean as you found them.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the condo.

Additional Guidelines

  • Dispose of trash in designated areas only.
  • Ensure doors and windows are locked when leaving the condo.
