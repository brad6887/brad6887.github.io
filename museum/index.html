Brad Cooke Presents

Museum of Dumb Ideas

A carefully curated collection of questionable projects, unnecessary domain purchases, abandoned plans, and ideas that were funny enough to preserve.

Curator's Note

Not every idea needs a business plan. Some ideas are useful. Some are terrible. Occasionally, one survives long enough to become something worthwhile.

The Museum exists to preserve all three.
